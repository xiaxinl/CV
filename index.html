Academic Projects

Northeastern University, Boston, MA

  • Project: Very Low Frequency (VLF) Magnetoelectric (ME) Antenna Array (June 2022 - Present)

    • Make compact size (0.7 cm3) ME resonators based on PZT/Metglas material that can transmit electromagnetic waves at the VLF range (21.1 kHz)
    • Solder circuits designed to tune each resonator in the array in phase at the same frequency.
    • Use a DC power supply, function generator, and spectrum analyzer for transmission and signal pick-up tests. The radiation field’s linear increase with the number of resonators in the array in operation. Achieved a total radiation field of 200 nT at 1 meter with 12 resonators with transmission distance up to ~ 100 meters
